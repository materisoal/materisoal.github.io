5. Contoh soal Expressing intention


Contoh 1 :

Fera: Hy Elvi!
(Hai Elvi!)
Elvi: Hy Fera!
(Hai Fera!)
Fera: Do you already have any plan for this holiday?
(Apa kamu sudah punya rencana untuk liburan kali ini?)
Elvi: Not really. How about you?
(Tidak juga. Bagaimana denganmu?)
Fera: I am planning to spend my holiday at my hometown.
(Aku berencana untuk menghabiskan waktu kiburan di kampung halamanku.)
Elvi: Your hometown? Where is it?
(Kampung halamanmu? Dimana itu?)
Fera: Lampung. I missed my hometown so much. It has been a year since the last time I visited my hometown.
(Lampung. Aku sangat merindukan kampung halamanku. Sudah satu tahun sejak terakhir kali aku mengunjunginya.)

Contoh 2 :

Adi: What are you doing Tio?
(Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan Tio?)
Tio: Nothing. I am just listening to music.
(Tidak ada. Aku hanya mendengarkan musik.)
Adi: Have you heard that Budi’s house was on fire?
(Sudahkah kamu mendengar bahwa rumah Budi kebakaran?)

Tio: Yes, I have.
(Ya, aku sudah dengar.)
Adi: Don’t you want to help him? I thought you two quite close.
(Apakah kamu tidak ingin menolongnya? Aku pikir kalian berdua cukup dekat.)
Tio: I intended to help him, but he said that he is fine and he doesn’t need my help.
(Aku bermaksud membantunya, tapi dia berkata kalau dia baik-baik saja dan tidak butuh bantuanku.)
Adi: He really said that?
(Dia benar-benar mengatakan itu?)
Tio: Yes, he did.
(Ya, dia mengatakannya.)
Adi: Maybe he got emotional and said that to you. Just let him alone for now.
(Mungkin dia menjadi emosional dan mengatakan hal seperti itu padamu. Biarkan saja dia sendiri untuk sekarang.)
Tio: Yes I know.
(Ya aku tahu.)

Contoh 3 :

Lia: Eva, have you heard that “Music Bank” concert will be held in Indonesia?
(Eva, sudahkah kamu mendengar bahwa konser “Music Bank” akan digelar di Indonesia?)
Eva: No, I haven’t heard that. Really?
(Belum, aku bekum dengar. Benarkah?)
Lia: Yes, really. I intend to save some money from now to buy the ticket.
(Ya, benar. Aku bermaksud untuk menabung dari sekarang untuk membeli tiketnya.)
Eva: I also will do it. So, we can watch the concert together.
(Aku juga akan menabunng. Jadi, kita bisa menonton kensernya bersama-sama.)
Lia: Yeah. That’s great!
(Ya. Itu bagus!)

12. kak tolong bantuin dong☺ buatkan contoh kalimat (masing" satu) dari expressing probability, expressing ability, expressing obligation and advice, expressing permission, dan expressing habits. terima kasih☺☺??


1. (Kemungkinan) Probability : "Maybe I could help you to service this software in your laptop. Because I know enough a lot about this software." Yang digaris bawahi ini sebagai kalimat pendukung untuk membuktikan bahwa kita mampu dan sekaligus meyakinkan orang.

2. (Kemampuan) Ability : "I can to completing the difficult task of writing this poem. So, I shall to completing it... Poetry is my specialization." Yang digaris bawahi ini juga sebagai kalimat pendukung dan meyakinkan, baik diri sendiri maupun orang lain 

3. (Kewajiban) Obligation : "As an obedient Muslim, I obliged to carry out the task of the five daily prayers of My Lord since birth." (Sebagai seorang Muslim yang taat, aku wajib melaksanakan tugas shalat lima waktu dari Tuhan-ku sejak lahir.)
    (Nasihat) Advice : "As your teacher, I suggest you to learn a lot more for the semester in Mathematics.. for the good of yourself." (Sebagai gurumu, aku sarankan bahwa kau harus belajar lebih banyak untuk Matematika... demi kebaikan dirimu sendiri.)

4. (Izin) Permission : "Well, I am going to give you permission to let out this room. For 2 minutes."

5. (Kebiasaan) Habits : "Yeah, boys. Reading has become my habit, since during my mother pregnancy of me; she diligently reading for me."

22. contoh modal expressing lack of necessity


Lack of necessity in the present:
- I don't have to run fast.
- I needn't buy all those things.

Lack of necessity in the past:
- I needn't have taken my jacket
- I didn't have to tell him about my homework. He already knew everything about it

Lack of necessity in the future:
- I won't have to take my umbrella. It won't be rain.
- I won't have to call on my mother tomorrow night. She'll be at the doctor's for her regular medical check up
