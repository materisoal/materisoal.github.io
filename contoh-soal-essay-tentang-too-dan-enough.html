TOO MUCH : Uncountable (tidak bisa dihitung)TOO MANY : Countable (dapat dihitung)

You put sugar too much

EXAMPLE 'ENOUGH'

22. pengertian too dan enough serta cara penggunaananya dan contohnya​


Too

1. Bisa diartikan 'juga'

* He read fictional book, I read fictional book too.

** Dia membaca buku fiksi, saya membaca buku fiksi juga.

2. Bisa diartikan 'sangat'

* The price of the book is too expensive

** Harga bukunya sangat mahal.

Enough

Artinya 'cukup'

* You were given enough time to finish your work.

** Kamu diberikan waktu yang cukup untuk menyelesaikan tugasmu.

30. buatlah contoh dialog menggukanan too dan enough


Jawaban:

A. Let's go to cinema. There is a new movie I like to see.

B. I think it is too early. It won't be open until 01:00 PM

C. Okay, we will wait. By the way it is an action movie.

Penjelasan:

Too dan enough merupakan kata yang menambahkan degree/derajat terhadap sebuah kata kerja, kata sifat atau kata keterangan.

dalam hal ini too bermakna terlalu (berlebihan) sedangkan enough bermakna cukup.

Contoh:

fast (cepat, kata sifat)

too fast (terlalu cepat) => too di sini menambahkan derajat makna terhadap kata fast

fast enough (cukup cepat)

kata lain yang kita bisa pakai untuk fungsi yang sama:

very (sangat), misalnya: very fast

almost (hampir) misalnya: almost fall asleep

31. Buat masing2 3 contoh kalimat menggunakan 1. Adjective enough 2. Adverb enough 3. Too adjective 4. Too adverb​


1. Adjective enough :

• The water isn't cold enough for me to swim in.

•This dress isn't long enough for the wedding.

•The cake wasn't sweet enough for my taste.

2. Adverb enough :

• He didn't run fast enough to catch the bus.

• She didn't speak loudly enough for the audience to hear.

• They didn't arrive early enough to get good seats.

3. Too adjective:

• The coffee is too hot to drink right now.

• The movie was too long and boring for me to enjoy.

• The music is too loud for me to concentrate.

4. Too adverb:

• She drove too fast on the highway and got a ticket.

• He talks too much during meetings and it's annoying.

• They danced too wildly and got kicked out of the club.
