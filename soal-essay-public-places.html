16. tolong buatkan evaluasi public places


MARKETING AND MARKETING PUBLIC RELATIONS (MPR)

In business activities that rely on marketing activities, sometimes a company is hindered from being able to enter a market or there is a rejection of products on the market.

This might happen because: the market does not need it anymore, either because of quality; price; improper distribution or promotion; often a product that has been well received in the market can fall in sales, not because of the presence of rivals but because its quality has suddenly declined.

This can result in the Product losing its image. In this kind of event, Public Relations is needed to bring back the image of the product in question.

From the illustrations of these cases, it can be understood that there is a connection between marketing and public relations (PR). In marketing activities there is a Formula 4P or known as Marketing mix, namely:

product

price

place

promotion

In this case Public Relations is in the element of promotion; which also includes advertising, direct marketing, personal selling and sales

》Descripting The Public Places

Public places (tempat-tempat umum) adalah berbagai sarana tempat yang dikhususkan bagi masyarakat umum dan sifatnya terbuka.

Contoh :

- rail station (stasiun kereta api)
- airport (bandar udara)
- bus station (terminal)
- market (pasar)
