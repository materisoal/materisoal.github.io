> ( JAWABAN TERCEDAS ) <

Relative Clause

5. Jasmine went to the bus stop. Her friends were waiting there.

Jasmine went to the bus stop where her friends were waiting.

6. The teacher told the naughty student to sit in front. She could keep an eye on him (there).

The teacher told the naughty student to sit in front where she could keep an eye on him.

7. This is the spot. The treasure is buried here.

This is the spot where the treasure is buried.

8. Mrs Nelson goes to the shop. She always buys her groceries there.

Mrs Nelson goes to the shop where she always buys her groceries.

---------------

Explication

Where adalah relative adverb yang digunakan untuk memperkenalkan relative clause yang menjelaskan suatu tempat.

Where menggantikan there/here/that place.

Contoh:

This is the house. I live here. ➯ This is the house where I live.

Relative adverb lainnya adalah when dan why.  

When digunakan untuk memperkenalkan relative clause yang menjelaskan suatu waktu.

Contoh:

August 17, 1945 was the day. We declared our independence then. ➯ August 17, 1945 was the day when we declared our independence.

Why memperkenalkan relative clause yang menjelaskan suatu alasan.

Contoh:

I have no idea. The reason he quits. ➯ I have no idea why he quits.

Penjelasan:

Adverb adalah kata keterangan atau kata tambahan yang memberikan gambaran lebih jelas dan detail untuk verb (kata kerja), adjective (kata sifat), atau adverb yang lainnya. Beberapa kata keterangan juga dapat memodifikasi frasa, klausa, atau bahkan memodifikasi keseluruhan kalimat.

Ada berbagai macam jenis adverb mulai dari adverb of time ( kata keterangan waktu ), adverb of place ( kata keterangan tempat ), adverb of manner, dan adverb of frequency.

Penggunaan adverb sebagai berikut

1. Adverb - Adjective

Contoh : His score is really good

Really disini adalah sebagai adverb dan good disini sebagai Adjective

2. Verb - Adverb

Contoh : She spoke loudly

Jadi spoke disini adalah bentuk past dari speak yang mana merupakan verb dan loudly disini adalah adverb

3. Adverb - sentence

Contoh : Finally, we get something to eat

Finally disini berfungsi sebagai adverb dan sentence atau kalimatnya adalah we get something to eat

13. soal descriptive text essay dan jawabanya tentang place


Descriptive Text

Pantai Galesong

Pantai Galesong is located in Makassar, South Sulawesi. It is 10 km away from Losari Beach and you will need 30 minutes by car to reach the place.

Even though Pantai Galesong is quite close to Pantai Losari, but it is not included in Makassar’s tourism destination. Instead, it is part of the Talakar districts, at desa Sampulungan Galesong to be precise.

Pantai Galesong is a great choice for family recreation, couples, community or even company gathering, training or celebrating anniversaries. There are many decent facilities such as spacious parking lot, decent guest houses with affordable prices, swimming pool for adults and children, ATV, moto cross, jetski, banana boat, traditional boat, outbond, cafes, etc.

For tourists who enjoy swimming in the sea could feel the warmth of the water in the morning. Life jacket will be provided there for rental. Visitors can also rent a traditional boat to explore the scenery of Pantai Galesong or even fishing.

Question and answer:

1. Where is Pantai Galesong located?

Answer: Pantai Galesong is located in Makassar, South Sulawesi.

2. How long does it take to reach there from Losari Beach?

Answer: You will need 30 minutes by car to reach the place.

3. What are facilities provided there?

Answer: Pantai Galesong provides decent facilities such as spacious parking lot, decent guest houses with affordable prices, swimming pool for adults and children, ATV, moto cross, jetski, banana boat, traditional boat, outbond, cafes, etc.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang Pantai Galesong di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.

Jawaban:

ADVERB OF MANNERShe is pacing around quickly. [Dia mondar-mandir dengan cepat]Tom and Chris are walking slowly. [Tom dan Chris berjalan dengan lambat]ADVERB OF PLACEWe are fishing over here. [Kami sedang memancing di sini]I am playing football with my friends there. [Saya sedang bermain sepak bola dengan teman-teman saya di sana]ADVERB OF TIMEThey are flying to Dubai today. [Mereka terbang ke Dubai hari ini]My father is working right now. [Ayahku sedang bekerja sekarang]

Penjelasan:

Present Continuous Tense dengan rumus umum:

[+] S + to be (is/am/are) + V.1 + -ing + ...

[-] S + to be (is/am/are) + not + V.1 + -ing + ...

[?] To be (Is/Am/Are) + S + V.1 + -ing + ...

KETERANGAN:

To be "is" dipakai untuk subjek: He, She, It, *nama seseorang*.To be "are" dipakai untuk subjek: I, You, We, They, atau subjek yang bersifat jamak (birds, Tom and Sara, etc).

above (di atas)

Have you read the explanation above? (Sudahkah kamu membaca penjelasan di atas?)

anywhere (di manapun, di mana saja)

Anywhere I go, I always bring my phone (Kemanapun aku pergi, aku selalu membawa ponselku)

away (jauh)

The butterflies fly away (Kupu-kupu itu terbang jauh)

back (kembali, ke belakang)

Go back and take all your things (Kembalilah dan ambil semua barangmu)

Doni took the book and hid it behind (Doni mengambil buku itu dan menyembunyikannya di belakang)

Answer the questions below correctly (Jawablah pertanyaan di bawah ini dengan tepat)

here (di sini)
