2. Past Continuos Tense adalah...Present continuos tense adalah ....​


Jawaban:

Past Continuos Tense adalah Kala imperfek menjelaskan tindakan yang tengah berlangsung pada masa lampau. Kalau seorang pengarang menggunakan kala imperfek, pasti mau menekankan keberlangsungan dari tindakan itu

Present continuous tense adalah tense yang berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan aksi yang sedang dilakukan pada masa sekarang (ketika berbicara) dan sesuatu di masa depan. Kalimat present continuous dibentuk oleh auxiliary verb “be” seperti am, is, are dan diikuti oleh present participle (bentuk V 1+ing).

Simple present tense
Formula:
1. (+) S + V1 + O (-) S + do not + V1 + O (?) do + S + V1 + O
2. (+) S (org ketiga tunggal: he, she, it, Ani or luqman) + V1s + O
(-) S + does not + V1 + O
(?) Does + S + V1 + O
3. (+) S+ to be (am/is/are) + non verb (-) S + to be + not + non verb (?) To Be + S + non verb

Examples
1. (+) you have a pen
(-) you do not have a pen
(?) do you have a pen?
2. (+) They go to school
(-) They do not go to school
(?) do they go to school?
3. (+) I and Nana buy new dresses
(-) I and Nana do not buy new dresses
(?) Do I and Nana buy new dresses?
4. (+) Alex and Doni play football
(-) Alex and doni do not play football
(?) Do Alex and Doni play football?

(?) does she make cakes?

(-) she is not a teacher
(?) is she a teacher?
9. (+) I am a student
(-) I am not a student
(?) are you a student?
10 (+) they are your friends
(-) they are not your friends
(?) are they your friends?

Present continuous tense
Formula
(+) S + to be (am/is/are) + Ving + O
(-) S + to be + not + Ving + O
(?) To be + S + Ving + O

23. rumus present tense dan present continuos


present tense = s+ v1+o
present continuos= s+ tobe+ V+ ing+ opresent tense:
• subject ( i, you, they, we)
(+) s + V1
(-) s + do + not + v1
(?) do + s + v1
• subject (she, he, it)
(+) s + v1(es/s)
(-) s + does + not + v1
(?) does + s + v1

present continous:
• subject (i, you, they, we, she, he, it)
(+) s + to be (is/am/are) + verb1-ing
(-) s + to be (is/am/are) + not + verb1-ing
(?) to be (is/am/are) + s + verb1-ing

25. rumus simple present continuos tense​


kalimat positif (+)

S + be (am/is/are) + present participle (-ing)

kalimat negatif (-)

S + be (am/is/are) + not + present participle (-ing)

kalimat interogatif (?)

be (am/is/are) + S + present participle (-ing)?

Present continuous tense atau yang biasa juga disebut dengan present progressive tense adalah tense yang menjelaskan pekerjaan yang sedang terjadi saat ini. Dikatakan present karena terjadi saat ini dan dikatakan continuous karena pekerjaannya sedang dilakukan (dalam progres).

rumus umum nya

(+) S + to be (is,am,are) + V ing

(-) S + to be (is,am,are) + not + V ing

(?) to be (is,am,are) + S + V ing?

(-) she is not charming

32. tolong diubah menjadi present continuos tense


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: Present continous tense
Pembahasan:

1. It is raining all week.
2. He is studying at the library. In fact, he is reviewing for the test everyday for last week.
3. Have you been doing exercise at the fitness centre? (yang lebih sesuai present perfect continous karena di kalimat pertama sudah menunjukkan efek dari 'doing exercise' yaitu 'look great'. Artinya kegiatan 'doing exercise' sudah dilakukan sebelum waktu sekarang)
4. We have been waiting for you since 1 pm. (yang lebih sesuai present perfect continous karena ada kata 'since', yang berarti 'sejak' artinya sudah menunggu sejak jam 1 tadi, atau kegiatan menunggu sudah dimulai beberapa saat yang lalu/ di masa lampau)
5. A car alarm is ringing somewhere down the street. It is driving me crazy. I wish it would stop. It has been ringing for more than twenty minutes. (yang lebih sesuai present perfect continous karena ada kata 'for' yang berarti selama, jadi alarm nya sudah berbunyi selama 20 menit, atau hal itu sudah terjadi sejak 20 menit yang lalu/ di masa lampau)

Jawaban:

Answer+Explain:

Present continuous tense adalah bentuk kalimat yang bertujuan untuk menunjukkan suatu keadaan/peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung sekarang/saat ini.

Rumus:

S + To be (Is/Am/Are) + Verb + (-ing) + Ket.

Contoh kalimat:

My father is working out of town right nowMy mom & my aunt is cooking right nowI am drawing a plane right nowMy sister is playing guitar right nowMy brother is playing football right nowMr. Andi is explaining in the classroom right nowdsb.

Note:

To be "Is" digunakan untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal, seperti dia (He/she), Mr. Andi, Mom, aunt, dsb.To be "Are" digunakan untuk subjek orang kedua (You) dan bentuk jamak.To be "Am" digunakan untuk subjek orang pertama tunggal (I).
