1. Buatlah Narrative Text berjudul Malin Kundang ?


A long time ago, in a small village near the beach in West Sumatra, a woman and her son lived. They were Malin Kundang and her mother. Her mother was a single parent because Malin Kundang's father had passed away when he was a baby. Malin Kundang had to live hard with his mother.

Malin Kundang was a healthy, dilligent, and strong boy. He usually went to sea to catch fish. After getting fish he would bring it to his mother, or sold the caught fish in the town. One day, when Malin Kundang was sailing, he saw a merchant's ship which was being raided by a small band of pirates. He helped the merchant. With his brave and power, Malin Kundang defeated the pirates. The merchant was so happy and thanked to him. In return the merchant asked Malin Kundang to sail with him. To get a better life, Malin Kundang agreed. He left his mother alone. Many years later, Malin Kundang became wealthy. He had a huge ship and was helped by many ship crews loading trading goods. Perfectly he had a beautiful wife too. When he was sailing his trading journey, his ship landed on a beach near a small village. The villagers recognized him. The news ran fast in the town; “Malin Kundang has become rich and now he is here”.

An old woman ran to the beach to meet the new rich merchant. She was Malin Kundang’s mother. She wanted to hug him, released her sadness of being lonely after so long time. Unfortunately, when the mother came, Malin Kundang who was in front of his well dressed wife and his ship crews denied meeting that old lonely woman. For three times her mother begged Malin Kundang and for three times he yelled at her. At last Malin Kundang said to her "Enough, old woman! I have never had a mother like you, a dirty and ugly woman!" After that he ordered his crews to set sail. He would leave the old mother again but in that time she was full of both sadness and angriness. Finally, enraged, she cursed Malin Kundang that he would turn into a stone if he didn't apologize. Malin Kundang just laughed and really set sail.

In the quiet sea, suddenly a thunderstorm came. His huge ship was wrecked and it was too late for Malin Kundang to apologize. He was thrown by the wave out of his ship. He fell on a small island. It was really too late for him to avoid his curse. Suddenly, he turned into a stone

4. contoh prosedur teks cerita Malin Kundang​


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh prosedur teks cerita "Malin Kundang":

Judul: Malin Kundang

Prosedur Teks:

Pendahuluan

Cerita "Malin Kundang" adalah sebuah legenda dari Indonesia yang telah diceritakan turun temurun. Cerita ini mengisahkan tentang seorang pemuda yang durhaka terhadap ibunya dan akhirnya dihukum menjadi batu oleh Tuhan.

Tokoh Utama

Malin Kundang: Pemuda tampan dan kuat yang durhaka kepada ibunya.Ibu Malin Kundang: Seorang ibu yang tulus mencintai anaknya.Kapal: Simbol kemakmuran dan kekayaan Malin Kundang.

Awal Cerita

Di sebuah desa nelayan di pantai Sumatera Barat, hiduplah seorang ibu tua yang mencintai anaknya, Malin Kundang, dengan sepenuh hati. Malin Kundang adalah pemuda yang tampan dan kuat. Ia bercita-cita menjadi pelaut yang kaya raya.

Konflik

Malin Kundang memutuskan untuk meninggalkan desanya dan pergi melaut dengan kapal besar. Ia berjanji akan kembali sebagai orang kaya dan sukses. Ibunya sangat sedih mendengar keputusan Malin Kundang, namun ia memberinya sebuah syal untuk menjaga hangat di laut.

Klimaks

Beberapa tahun kemudian, Malin Kundang telah menjadi seorang pelaut kaya. Ia memiliki kapal besar dan harta melimpah. Namun, ia telah melupakan janjinya untuk kembali ke desa dan menjenguk ibunya.

Puncak Konflik

Suatu hari, kapal Malin Kundang berlabuh di pantai desanya. Ibunya mendengar kabar bahwa anaknya telah pulang sebagai seorang yang kaya dan sukses. Ia sangat bahagia dan segera pergi ke pantai untuk menemui Malin Kundang.

Penyelesaian

Ketika ibunya mencapai pantai dan berusaha mendekati Malin Kundang, ia menolak untuk mengakui ibunya. Ia malah memerintahkan awak kapalnya untuk pergi. Ibunya yang sedih sangat terluka dan merasa ditolak oleh anaknya sendiri.

Akhir Cerita

Ketika Malin Kundang menolak ibunya, tiba-tiba badannya berubah menjadi batu besar. Ia dihukum oleh Tuhan karena durhaka kepada ibunya. Kapalnya juga menjadi batu besar. Pantai tempat ini kini dikenal sebagai "Batu Malin Kundang" dan menjadi pelajaran tentang pentingnya menghormati orang tua.

Penutup

Cerita Malin Kundang mengajarkan kita pentingnya menghormati orang tua dan mengingat asal-usul kita. Durhaka kepada orang tua dapat mendatangkan nasib buruk, seperti yang dialami oleh Malin Kundang dalam cerita ini.

Malin Kundang

Once upon a time, on the north coast of Sumatera lived a poor woman and his son, who called Malin Kundang. His Father eventually died, and his mother lived alone as a poverty-stricken old woman.

Malin Kundang grew up as a skillful young boy. He always helps his mother to earn some money. One day, Malin Kundang decide going to overseas and promise to come back.

After several years had gone by, Malin Kudang finally decide to return to his village. He arrived wearing fine clothes and traveling on one of his ships. Someone arriving in such splendor was uncommon to the villagers, so many of them went down to the harbor to view the sight.

One of the villagers recognized Malin Kundang form a scar that he had received while playing as a child. Upon recognizing the mark, the villager went to tell Malin Kundang's mother that her son had returned. Excitedly, she went to the shore and recognized her son the minute her eyes fell on him.

When the older woman called him her son, he refused to believe that he head such an old woman as a mother. His disbelief was heightened when his wife questioned why he had not told her that he had an elderly, poor mother.

In an alternate translation, Malin Kundang was on the ship with just his crew. When his mother attempted to embrace him, he was too embarrassed by her ragged appearance to acknowledge her and instead, had one of them carry her away.

Distraught and finally realizing he son's wickedness, Malin Kundang's mother gave up on her son's acknowledging her and prayed to her god to punish her son for his behavior.

The day after his mother's prayer, Malin Kudang sailed out of the village. Shortly thereafter, the ships was met by a violent storm. Malin Kundang believed the storm was his god's and nature's ways of punishing him for his mistreatment of his mother.

He felt guilty about his behavior towards her, asked for forgiveness, and began to pray. However, his repentance was too late, for the ship was destroyed at sea. But, in some translations of the tale, Malin Kundang was turned into coral. In other, the ship, the crew, and he become rock formations that are still standing.

THE LEGEND OF MALIN KUNDANG

{Orientation}

A long time ago, in a small village near the beach in West Sumatra lived a woman and her son, Malin Kundang. Malin Kundang and his mother had to live hard because his father had passed away when he was a baby. Malin Kundang was a healthy, dilligent, and strong boy. He usually went to sea to catch fish. After getting fish he would bring it to his mother, or sell the caught fish in the town.

{Complication}

One day, when Malin Kundang was sailing, he saw a merchant’s ship being raided by a band of pirates. With his bravery, Malin Kundang helped the merchant defeat the pirates. To thank him, the merchant allowed Malin Kundang to sail with him. Malin Kundang agreed in the hope to get a better life. He left his mother alone.

Many years later, Malin Kundang became wealthy. He had a huge ship and a lot of crews who worked loading trading goods. He was also married to a beautiful woman. When he was sailing on his trading journey, his ship landed on a coast near a small village. The local people recognized that it was Malin Kundang, a boy from the area. The news ran fast in the town; “Malin Kundang has become rich and now he is here”. An old woman, who was Malin Kundang’s mother, ran to the beach to meet the new rich merchant. She wanted to hug him to release her sadness of being lonely after a long time. When his mother came near him, Malin Kundang who was with his beautiful wife and his ship crews denied that she was his mother. She had pleaded Malin Kundang to look at her and admit that she was her mother. But he kept refusing to do it and yelling at her. At last Malin Kundang said to her “Enough, old woman! I have never had a mother like you, a dirty and ugly woman!” After that he ordered his crews to set sail to leave the old woman who was then full of sadness and anger.

{Resolution}

Finally, feeling enraged, she cursed Malin Kundang that he would turn into a stone if he didn’t apologize to her. Malin Kundang just laughed and set sail. Suddenly a thunderstorm came in the quiet sea, wrecking his huge ship. He was thrown out to a small island. It was really too late for him to avoid his curse; he had turned into a stone.

34. apa saja karakter malin kundang,ibu malin kundang,dan isteri malin kundang


Karakter Malin Kundang adalah antagonis. Karakter Ibu Malin Kundang adalah protagonis. Sedangkan karakter istri Malin Kundang adalah tritagonis.

Pembahasan :

Malin Kundang adalah cerita rakyat Sumatera Barat yang sudah terkenal di Indonesia. Kisah ini mengajarkan kita untuk berbakti kepada orang tua, khususnya Ibu. Kita tidak boleh menyakiti hatinya.

Ringkasan cerita :

Pada zaman dahulu hiduplah Malin dan Ibunya di kampung tepi pantai. Ketika Malin sudah besar, Ia merantau untuk mencari kehidupan yang lebih layak. Pada suatu hari Ibunya mendengar berita bahwa Malin akan pulang ke kampung. Ia berdiri di tepi pantai menyambut Malin. Namun sayang, Malin tidak mengakui Ibunya di depan istri dan pegawai-pegawainya. Akhirnya Ibu Malin sakit hati. Ia berdoa kepada Tuhan untuk mengubah Malin, istri, pegawai, dan kapalnya menjadi batu. Ternyata doa seorang Ibu sangat ampuh. Malin, istri, pegawai, dan kapalnya berubah menjadi batu.

Sekarang, kita akan menganalisis tokoh dan karakter dalam kisah tersebut. Tokoh adalah pelaku yang memerankan adegan dalam cerita. Karakter tokoh dibagi menjadi tiga. Yaitu :

Protagonis, tokoh yang baik. Dalam kisah tersebut adalah Ibu Malin karena sudah membesarkan Malin dan mengakui anaknya.Antagonis, tokoh yang jahat. Dalam kisah tersebut adalah Malin karena durhaka dan tidak mengakui Ibunya.Tritagonis, tokoh yang berada di antara baik dan jahat. Dalam kisah tersebut adalah istri, pegawai-pegawai Malin.

38. Mengapa ibu Malin Kundang mengutuk Malin Kundang


Ibu Malin Kundang mengutuk Malin Kundang karena Ia durhaka dan tidak mengakui Ibunya.

Pembahasan :

Malin Kundang adalah cerita rakyat dari Minangkabau, Sumatera Barat. Kisah ini mengajarkan anak-anak agar tidak durhaka kepada orang tua, khususnya Ibu. Karena do'a seorang Ibu sangatlah ampuh. Jika Ibu sakit hati lalu mendo'akan yang buruk, maka bisa saja itu menjadi kenyataan. Konon batu Malin Kundang masih ada di tepi Pantai Air Manis.

Ringkasan cerita :

Pada zaman dahulu hiduplah Malin dan Ibunya di kampung tepi pantai. Ketika Malin sudah besar, Ia merantau untuk mencari kehidupan yang lebih layak. Pada suatu hari Ibunya mendengar berita bahwa Malin akan pulang ke kampung. Ia berdiri di tepi pantai menyambut Malin. Namun sayang, Malin tidak mengakui Ibunya di depan istri dan pegawai-pegawainya. Akhirnya Ibu Malin sakit hati. Ia berdoa kepada Tuhan untuk mengubah Malin, istri, pegawai, dan kapalnya menjadi batu. Ternyata doa seorang Ibu sangat ampuh. Malin, istri, pegawai, dan kapalnya berubah menjadi batu.

Dalam cerita tersebut, Ibu Malin Kundang mengutuk Malin Kundang menjadi batu karena Ia durhaka dan tidak mengakui Ibunya.

39. contoh teks naratif. dalam cerita Malin Kundang ​


Jawaban:

Malin Kundang adalah seorang anak muda yang tinggal di sebuah desa nelayan di pantai Sumatera Barat. Dia berasal dari keluarga miskin dan tinggal bersama ibunya, seorang janda yang bekerja keras sebagai penjual ikan. Malin Kundang bercita-cita tinggi, dia ingin menjadi seorang pelaut hebat dan menjelajahi lautan luas.

Suatu hari, sebuah kapal besar datang ke desa mereka. Kapal itu dikomandani oleh seorang kapten yang melihat potensi besar dalam Malin Kundang. Kapten tersebut mengajak Malin Kundang bergabung dengan kru kapalnya dan berlayar ke dunia yang jauh. Malin Kundang dengan senang hati menerima tawaran itu dan meninggalkan desa pantainya.

Bertahun-tahun berlalu, Malin Kundang menjadi seorang pelaut berpengalaman dan kapten kapalnya sendiri. Dia mengarungi lautan luas dan menjadi seorang yang kaya raya. Namun, kekayaannya membuatnya lupa akan akar dan keluarganya yang tinggal di desa miskin.

Suatu hari, kapal Malin Kundang berlabuh di pantai dekat desanya. Ibu Malin Kundang mendengar kabar kedatangannya dan langsung pergi ke pantai untuk menyambut anaknya. Namun, ketika melihat Malin Kundang yang kaya dan gagah, dia merasa sedih dan kecewa karena Malin Kundang telah melupakan asal-usulnya.

Dalam kemarahannya, ibu Malin Kundang berbicara keras, mengutuk anaknya yang sombong dan menyuruhnya kembali ke laut. Ketika Malin Kundang melihat ibunya, dia marah dan menolak permintaannya. Dia berbalik dan bersumpah bahwa dia tidak akan pernah mengakui ibunya lagi.

Tidak lama setelah itu, badai mengerikan melanda perairan dan kapal Malin Kundang terombang-ambing. Malin Kundang berjuang keras untuk bertahan, tapi semua usahanya sia-sia. Saat kapalnya hancur, Malin Kundang terpental ke pantai. Dia terluka parah dan berusaha merangkak menuju ibunya yang masih berdiri di pantai. Namun, saat dia mencapai pantai, dia tiba-tiba berubah menjadi batu besar.

Konon, Malin Kundang dihukum oleh tuhan laut karena sifatnya yang sombong dan tidak menghormati ibunya. Batu besar tersebut masih berdiri di pantai hingga sekarang dan dikenal sebagai Batu Malin Kundang, sebagai peringatan akan akibat perilaku sombong dan lupa akan asal-usul.
