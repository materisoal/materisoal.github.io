17. Berikan contoh expressing offers and suggestion


A. Suggestion

a. Definition

Suggestion (saran) adalah mengajukan suatu

rencana untuk dipertimbangkan atau memberikan/menyumbangkan suatu ide. Hal-hal yang disarankan dapat diterima atau ditolak. Suggestion bersifat abstrak, dan hal-hal yang bisa disarankan seperti solution (solusi), advice (nasehat), plan (rencana), dan idea (ide).

b. Expressions Suggestion :

- Let's ...

- Why don't we ...

- We could ...

- What about ...

- I suggest that ...

- You might want to change ...

- I think ...

- I don't think ...

c. Responding Suggestion :

Accepting Suggestion

- Yes, let's go

- Ok, I will

- It's a good idea

- Okay, if you say so

- Let's

Declining Suggestion

- No, thank you. I don't feel like going

- Sorry, I think ...

- No, let's just do something else

- No, let's just go somewhere else

- Sorry, I can't. I have another appointment

- No, sorry.

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Let's go to the beach this weekend!

B : It's a good idea. Let's go!

- Dialogue 2 :

A : What don't you study for the test instead of playing outside?

B : Okay, if you say so

- Dialogue 3 :

A : I think you should go apologize to her

B : Sorry, I can't. It's her fault

- Dialogue 4 :

A : You might want to change your mind

B : No, sorry, I've planned this for months

B. Offers

a. Definition

Offers (tawaran) adalah memberikan sesuatu kepada seseorang sebagai hadiah (gifts), pertukaran (trade) atau bantuan (help). Pada umumnya, offers bersifat nyata (konkrit), dan hal-hal yang dapat diberikan kepada seseorang sebagai offers seperti food (makanan), money (uang), solution (solusi), friendship (persahabatan), atau bargain (penawaran).

b. Expression Offers :

- May I ...

- Can I ...

- Shall I ...

- Would you ...

- How about I ...

c. Responding Offers :

Accepting Offers

- Yes, please. I really appreciate it

- Thank you, it's very kind of you

- Yes, please, that be lovely

- Yes, please, that would be very kind of you

- Thank you, I appreciate your help

Declining Offers

- It's okay, I can do it myself

- No, thank you

- No, thanks

- Don't worry, I will do it myself

- That's alright, I will manage it on my own

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Can I help you?

B : Yes, please, I really appreciate your help

- Dialogue 2 :

A : Could I offer you something?

B : Yes, please, I want a cup of tea

- Dialogue 3 :

A : Would you care for another cup of tea?

B : No, thank you.

- Dialogue 4 :

A : Can I clean the car for you?

B : It's okay, I can do it myself

23. pengertian dan fungsi expressing of suggestion dalam bahasa inggris


Percakapan giving advice atau giving suggestion merupakan sebuah pola kalimat di dalam bahasa inggris yang digunakan untuk memberikan nasihat atau saran kepada seseorang. Bentuk kalimat seperti ini seringkali kita temukan di dalam percakapan sehari-hari. Namun, di dalam bahasa inggris ada aturan tersendiri ketika kita ingin menyampakan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain. Oleh karena itu, pada kesempatan ini kami akan membahas mengenai contoh percakapan giving advice atau giving suggestion dalam bahasa inggris.Untuk memberikan saran/nasihat didalam bahasa inggris, kita dapat menggunakan pola yang biasa digunakan seperti berikut ini:You had better….. (kamu lebih baik…..)
You Should….. (kamu harus….)
I advise you to….. (saya menyarankan kamu untuk…..)
You should not….. (kamu seharusnya tidak…..)
My advice is….. (saran saya adalah…..)
….ought to….. (…….seharusnya……)
I’d suggest….. (saya menyarankan…..)

Aqila : I'm fine, too. Where will you do tomorrow?

Hanna : I have to finish my math homework tomorrow or my teacher will scolding me. Do you have any suggestion to finish them easily? Because I'm not good at math. It's too difficult.

Aqila : Really? Oh, I think you should do it with your friends so you don't have any difficulties. Or maybe you could take some private math tutor to help you

Hanna : Yes, you're right. But I have to submit my homework tomorrow. Is it possible for me to take private lessons as fast as that?

Aqila : You could take the tutor from the online private tutoring

Hanna : Good idea! What if I'll do my homework myself? I don't have any good internet connection.

Aqila : No, don't do it yourself. If you don't want your values go bad, don't do it yourself. You can come to my house to borrow my internet connection. I'm using Wi-Fi, anyway. And we could

Hanna : Woah, thanks for your kindness, Aqila!

Hanna : Okay. Let's go then.


-----

Obligation's expression :
I have to finish my math homework tomorrow or my teacher will scolding me.

Suggestion's expression :
I think you should do it with your friends so you don't have any difficulties.

Prohibition's expression :
No, don't do it yourself. If you don't want your values go bad, don't do it yourself.
