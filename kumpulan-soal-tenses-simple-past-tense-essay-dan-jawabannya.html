1.Pada dasarnya, simple present tersebut dapat kita artikan sebagai bentuk kata kerja yang paling sederhana dari jenis tense lainnya. Kata Kerja ini digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari, berulang seperti rutinitas atau kebiasaan. Ini menjadi salah satu tenses yang paling sering dipakai dalam bahasa Inggris.

2.Simple past tense adalah kalimat tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan telah berakhir di masa lampau. Berbeda dengan past continous tense, yakni menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau, namun masih terjadi di masa sekarang.

* simple present tense : subject + to be+ object

exp. The children are naughty.

jika menjadi kalimat negatif maka ditambahkan Not dibelakang to be.

exp. the children are not/aren't naughty

* simple future tense : Subject + will/shall + Verb 1 + Object

exp. I will come to your house. (hal yg pasti / "pasti datang")

bentuk negatif dari "will" adalah "would" dan bentuk negatif dari "Shall" adalah "should"

exp. Abdul went to Bali for holiday last Sunday

untuk kalimat negatifnya menggunakan rumus

S + did + not + Verb 1

atau

S + To Be (Was / Were) + not

exp. I did not/din't sleep well last night

Dalam kalimat simple past tense, kata kerja / verb yang digunakan merupakan bentuk kata kerja kedua. Terdapat 2 jenis kata kerja / Verb, yakni Regular Verb dan Irregular Verb. Untuk regular verb, tambahkan -ed / -d dibelakang setelah kata kerja bentuk pertama. Sebagai contoh

Stay -> stayed (Tinggal)

Punch -> Punched (Memukul)

Play -> Played (Bermain)

Touch -> Touched (Menyentuh)

Write -> Writed (Menulis)

Penjelasan:

1. Simple present tense:

A. Verbal Sentence:

(+) S + V1 (+ s/es) + complement (C) or object (O)

ex: • she plays basketball

• they play basketball

(-) S + don't/doesn't + V1 + C/O

ex: • she doesn't play basketball

• they don't play basketball

(?) do/does + S + V1 + C/O

ex: • does she play basketball?

• do they play basketball?

B. Nominal Sentence:

(+) S + are/is/am + C

ex: • he is clever

• they are busy

(-) S + are/is/am + not + C

ex: • he is not clever

• they are not busy

(?) are/is/am + S + C

ex: • is he clever?

• are they busy?

2. Simple Past Tense:

A. Verbal Sentence:

(+) S + V2 + C/O

ex: • she cooked noodle yesterday

• they bought a new DVD 3 weeks ago

(-) S + did not/didn't + V1 + C/O

ex: • she did not cook noodle yesterday

• they did not buy a new DVD 3 weeks ago

(?) did + S + V1 + C/O

ex: • did she cook noodle yesterday?

• did they buy a new DVD 3 weeks ago?

B. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + was/were + C

ex: • he was so happy last night

• they were so sad yesterday

(-) S + was/were + not + C

ex: • he was not so happy last night

• they were not so sad yesterday

(?) was/were + S + C

ex: • was he so happy last night?

• were they so sad yesterday?

3. Simple Future Sentence

A. Verbal Sentence:

(+) S + shall/will + V1 + C/O

ex: • she will fly to Seoul next week

• they will make a farewell party next month

(-) S + will/shall + not + V1 + C/O

ex: • she will not fly to Seoul next week

• they will not make a farewell party next

month

(?) will/shall + S + V1 + C/O

ex: • will she fly to Seoul next week?

• will they make a farewell party next month?

B. Nominal Sentence:

(+) S + will/shall + be + C

ex: • they will be at your house this morning

• i will be a doctor 20 years later

(-) S + will/shall + not + be + C

ex: • they will not be at your house this morning

• i will not be a doctor 20 years later

(?) will/shall + S + be + C

ex: • will they be at your house this morning?

• will you be a doctor 20 years later?

Additional:

1. kalimat verbal digunakan jika predikatnya kata kerja, sedangkan kalimat nominal digunakan jika predikatnya bukan kata kerja.

2. dalam British English, kata “shall” digunakan jika subjeknya adalah “I” dan “we”, sedangkan dalam American English, semua kalimat future biasa menggunakan kata will.

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

 

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils ?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?  

Past Perfect Tense

Past perfect tense adalah suatu tense yang menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau dan telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain yang terjadi di masa lampau juga. Tense ini terdiri dari gabungan aspek perfect (sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau) dan bingkai waktu past (masa lampau).

Rumus Past Perfect Tense

Tense ini dibentuk dengan bentuk masa lampau dari auxiliary verb have atau has, yaitu had dan past participle (verb 3). Semua subjek baik singular atau plural menggunakan had.

Positif

Subjek +  had + verb 3  

Contoh

They had lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had learned English for one year

Negatif

Subjek +  had + not + verb 3  

Contoh:

They had not lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had not learned English for one year

Interogatif

Had+ subjek + verb 3?  

Contoh:

Had they lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time Had he learned English for one year?
