4. percakapan asking for permission and the response


Asking for Permission

1. Can I …?

Can I go with Mary tonight? (Bolehkah aku pergi dengan Mary malam ini)

Can I borrow your pen? (Bisakah kupinjam penamu?)

Can I try? (Bolehkah aku mencoba/mencicipi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “can” dalam permission sangat informal, bisa pula terkesan kurang sopan. Jadi, ada baiknya hanya digunakan dalam pembicaraan dengan orang-orang terdekat.

2. May I …?

May I have another cup of coffee? (Bolehkah saya minta secangkir kopi lagi)

May I see your daughter? (Bolehkah saya menemui anak perempuan anda?)

May I go to the bathroom? (Bolehkah saya pergi ke kamar mandi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “may” untuk saat ini terbilang lebih baku; baiknya digunakan dalam percakapan atau urusan formal.

3. Could I …?

Could I use the phone please? (Bisakah saya memakai teleponnya?)

Could I please hold the baby? (Bolehkah saya menimang bayinya?)

Could I eat this pizza? (Bolehkah saya makan pizza ini?)

5. 5 contoh kata asking for permission ( meminta ijin ) ?


ASKING PERMISSION (EXPRESSION)Jawaban Pendahuluan

Expression asking permission adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk menanyakan persetujuan kepada orang lain mengenai hal yang akan dilakukan atau ijin untuk menggunakan barang milik orang lain, seringkali diakhiri dengan kata please, untuk menunjukkan kesopanan. Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam asking permission misalnya :

Can I...

Would you mind...

May I ...

Can you please..

I wonder if I could

Phrase yang digunakan untuk menerima/giving permission

Yes, you can

Sure, Go ahead

Sure

I don't mind

Please feel free

Phrase yang digunakan untuk menolak/refusing permission

No, you can't

I'm sorry, you can't

It's not possible

I'm afraid not

Pembahasan

Contoh Kalimat Asking Permission

1. Can I borrow your book?

   Respond : Yes , you can

                      I'm afraid I'm reading the book right now.

2. Would you mind if I turn on the AC?

    Respond : I don't mind.

                      Sorry, but I already feel cold.

3. May I know your name?

   Respond : Yes, sure. My name is Felicia.

4. I wonder if I could stay with you for a week.

  Respond : Yes, that would be fine.

                     I'm sorry but I would be away for a week.

5. Is it OK if I come earlier?

   Respond : Yes, that would be OK

                      I'm afraid you have to wait outside if you come earlier.

Kesimpulan

Dalam beberapa kalimat contoh yang dibuat kita menggunakan Can, Would you mind, May, I wonder... dan Is  it Ok, beserta kemungkinan kita meresponnya, baik menerima atau menolak memberi ijin.

How to Ask for Permission in English

Can I + verb (very informal)

Can I go out tonight?

Can he have dinner with us?

NOTE: The use of "Can I do something?" is very informal, and considered incorrect by many. However, it is used in everyday informal speech and for that reason has been included.

May I + verb

May I have another piece of pie?

May we go out with our friends tonight?

NOTE: Traditionally, the use of "May I do something?" has been used for asking permission. In modern society, this form has become a little more formal and is often replaced with other forms such as "Can I..." and "Could I ..." Many argue that "Can I ..." is incorrect because it refers to ability. However, this form is quite common in everyday, spoken situations.

Could I please + verb

Could I please go with Tom to the movie?

Could we please go on trip this weekend?

Do you think I could + verb

Do you think I could use your cell phone?

Do you think I could borrow your car?

Would it be possible for me + infinitive

Would it be possible for me to use your computer for a few minutes?

Would it be possible for to study in this room?

Would you mind if I + verb in past

Would you mind if I stayed a few more minutes?

Would you mind if I took a five minute break?

How to Grant Permission in English

If you would like to say "yes" to someone who asks permission, you can give permission using these phrases. The first three are more informal, while the fourth is formal.

Sure.

No problem.

Go right ahead.

Please feel free + infinitive

How to Politely Refuse a Favor/Deny Permission

Saying 'no', is never fun, but sometimes it's necessary. See the conversations below for some examples.

I'm afraid I'd prefer if you didn't / don't.

Sorry, but I'd rather you not do that.

Unfortunately, I need to say no.

I'm afraid that's not possible.

When denying permission, people will sometimes instead offer to help in other ways, using the words "how about" and "instead" to offer alternatives.

I'm afraid I can't let you borrow my car, but I could drive you instead.

I can't babysit your daughter. How about I call my sitter for you instead?

I wish I could help out; maybe another time.

27. 1 contoh asking for permission


Excuse me, may I have a seatContoh Kalimat Asking Permission (Meminta izin) and Prohibition (Larangan)

Berikut adalah beberapa frase dan contoh kalimat yang bisa digunakan untuk meminta izin kepada orang lain (asking permission) dan larangan (prohibition).


A.        Asking Permission (Meminta izin)


1.         Menggunakan Nouns: Permission (Minta izin)


Contoh Kalimat:

I beg you permission to enter the room.

(Artinya: Saya minta izin anda untuk memasuki ruangan)

The teacher gives me permission to go out.

(Artinya: Guru memberi saya izin untuk keluar)

Etc.


2.         Menggunakan Verbs: Permit, let, allow (Izin, membiarkan)


Contoh Kalimat:

My mother permits me to stay at home alone.

(Artinya: Ibuku mengizinkanku untuk tinggal di rumah sendiri)

Allow me to pick you up.

(Artinya: Izinkan saya menjemputmu)

Etc.


3.         May I (Bolehkah saya …)


Contoh Kalimat:

May I borrow your phone, please?

(Artinya: Bolehkah saya meminjam telepon kamu?)

May I sit down?

(Artinya: Bolehkah saya duduk?)

Etc.


4.         Do you mind + Gerund (Apakah kamu keberatan)


Contoh Kalimat:

Do you mind accepting my idea?

(Artinya: Apakah kamu menerima gagasanku?)

Do you mind staying here one night?

(Artinya: Apakah kamu tidak keberatan untuk tinggal di sini semalam?)

Etc.
