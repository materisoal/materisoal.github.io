ANSWER

1. BUY

present tense= i buy a book

past= I was buy a book

future= I will buy a book

2. TAKE

present tense= She takes a bus to school

past= She took a bus to school

future= She will take a bus to school

present tense= I get a fever now.

past= I got a fever

future= I will get a fever

EXPLANATION

Rumus SIMPLE Present Tense :

(+) S + Verb -s/es + O

Rumus Simple Past Tense :

(+) S + Verb2 + O atau,(+) S + was/were + Complement

Rumus Simple Future Tense :

(+) S + will/shall + Verb1

Rumus Present Continuous Tense :

(+) S + to be (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Complement

9. Essay: Translate into English using: only (+) a. Simple Present Tense b. Present Continuous Tense c. Simple Past Tense d. Present Prefect Tense e. Future Tense "Tono dan aku menggali sebuah lubang di kebun."​


Jawaban:

a. Simple Present Tense

Tono and I dig a hole in the garden

b. Present Continuous Tense

Tono and I are digging a hole in the garden

c. Simple Past Tense

Tono and I dug a hole in the garden

d. Present Prefect Tense

Tono and I have dug a hole in the garden

e. Future Tense

Tono and I will dig a hole in the garden

Penjelasan:

Saya asumsikan kamu sudah faham rumus dari setiap tense.

Perhatikan:

Tono and I adalah subjek kalimat.

Tono and I berarti kami, we, jamak.

Karena subjeknya jamak, dalam present tense (baik simple present, present continuous dan present perfect) gunakan bentuk verb dan to be yang sesuai.

Misalnya dalam simple present tense, gunakan dig, bukan digs.

Kecuali subjeknya hanya Tono (he) maka kata kerjanya ditambah s, jadi digs

Begitu juga dalam present continuous tense maka gunakan are digging, bukan is digging.

Seandainya subjeknya hanya Tono, maka akan ditulis Tono is digging...

Begitu juga dalam present perfect tense, gunakak have dug, bukan has dug

Seandainya subjeknya hanya Tono, maka akan ditulis Tono has dug...

Note:

dig (menggali) adalah kata kerja irregular

verb 1: dig

verb 2: dug => dalam kalimat simple past tense

verb 3: dug (penulisan sama dengan verb 2) => dalam kalimat present perfect tense

Semoga bermanfaat

21. soal dan jawaban tentang past tense vs present perfect


Subject : English  

Category :Tenses  

Sub-category : Present perfect tense

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

--------

Present perfect tense dan past simple tense adalah salah dua dari 16 tense dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Present perfect digunakan untuk kejadian yang dimulai pada suatu waktu yang tidak diketahui di masa lampau dan 'sempurna' di masa sekarang atau hasilnya terasa/terlihat/ada sekarang.

Konstruksi : S + have/has + past participle (v₃)

Penanda : already, yet, never, ever, since ..., for ..., just ..., ... times, ada jumlah, dll.

-----------

Past simple digunakan untuk kejadian yang terjadi dan selesai di suatu waktu spesifik di masa lampau.

Konstruksi : S + preterite (v₂)

Penanda : yesterday, last ..., ... ago, just now, this morning, dll.

--------

Contoh soal dan jawaban :

1. The Titanic ..............sank.................... (sink) in 1912.

2. Dolphins ............have lived................. (live) in BC waters for millions of years, and are still common today.

3. I ............wore...................... (wear) these boots to go hiking yesterday.

4. Jennie .........has never eaten............ (never, eat) bacon in her life! She's a true vegetarian.

5. I ..........have bought........ (buy) some new shorts, but I don't think I like them. Maybe I'll take them back to the store.

6. We .............have known............. (know) each other since we.......were.......... (be) children.

7. Peter ..........has called........ (call) you three times already. He ..........called........ (call) again just now.

38. yang digunakan pada 7 tenses -present tense -present contineous -past tense -past contineous -present ferpect -past ferpect -future tense Tolong dong dibantu


TENSES

Present Tense :

S + verb-1/be (am/is/are)

Present Continuous :

S + am/is/are + V(+ing)

Past Tense :

S + verb-2/be (was/were)

Past Continuous :

S + was/were + V(+ing)

Present Perfect :

S + have/has + verb 3

Past Perfect :

S + had + Verb-3

Future Tense :

- S + will + bare infinitive

- S + be (is/am/are) + going to + bare infinitive)

NB : Pada future tense will digunakan saat dalam keadaan spontan (tidak terencana) dan going to digunakan saat dalam keadaan sudah direncanakan
